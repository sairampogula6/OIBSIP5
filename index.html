SP BALASUBRAHMANYAM

BIOGRAPHY

**S.P. Balasubrahmanyam (1946-2020):**

- S.P. Balasubrahmanyam, born on June 4, 1946, in Nellore, India, was a legendary Indian playback singer, music director, and actor.
- His illustrious career spanned over five decades, during which he sang in numerous Indian languages and recorded over 40,000 songs.
- Known for his melodious and versatile voice, he worked with renowned composers and won several awards, including National Film Awards and Filmfare Awards.
- He made a mark in both the music and film industry, and his iconic songs remain timeless.
- S.P. Balasubrahmanyam passed away on September 25, 2020, leaving behind a lasting legacy in Indian music and cinema.
